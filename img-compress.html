Frequently Asked Questions

Is this image compressor free to use?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges or watermarks.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can choose the output format or let our tool select the optimal format automatically.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. We never upload your images to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.
